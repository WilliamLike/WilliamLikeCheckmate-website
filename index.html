About Me

Hello mga Ka-Checkmate, I'm William and I recently completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Bicol University. One of my main endeavors has been streaming chess, and I embarked on this journey with multiple objectives in mind. Firstly, my intention was to share my immense passion for the game of chess with a wider audience. By streaming, I sought to create a platform where I could engage with fellow enthusiasts and foster a community centered around this intellectually stimulating and captivating game.

Moreover, my goal extended beyond simply streaming games. I aimed to develop a vibrant chess community where players of all skill levels could come together to learn, discuss strategies, and appreciate the intricacies of the game. Through interactive sessions, I strived to encourage viewers to actively participate, share their insights, and collectively enhance our understanding of chess.

Additionally, teaching and mentoring children became a significant part of my streaming journey. I firmly believe that teaching is a powerful way to deepen one's own understanding, and I wanted to employ this principle while introducing chess to young minds. By presenting the game in a fun and engaging manner, I aimed to make chess accessible to children, providing them with an avenue to learn and grow their analytical skills, critical thinking abilities, and decision-making prowess.

Overall, my streaming endeavors were driven by the belief that by teaching others, we not only contribute to their growth but also enhance our own knowledge and expertise. Through my streams, I aspired to create a positive and educational space, where viewers could embrace their love for chess, connect with a passionate community, and experience the joy of both playing and learning.
